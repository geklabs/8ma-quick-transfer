## Short pitch

Free browser file transfers between phones and computers, with no account or app installation.

## Full description

8ma Quick Transfer helps you move a file from one device to another without installing an app or creating an account. Choose a file and share a temporary link, QR code, or 8-character pickup code with the receiver.

For devices on the same WiFi, open the site on both screens and use nearby sharing. Both pages stay open during the transfer. The service is free, and desktop Chrome and Edge support files up to 100 GB. Actual speed depends on the devices and network.
