## Short description

Send large files between computers and phones in a browser, with no account or app installation.

## Full description

8ma Quick Transfer moves files between phones and computers in the browser. The sender picks a file and shares a temporary link, QR code, or 8-character pickup code. Devices on the same WiFi can also discover a nearby share after opening the site.

The service is free and does not require an account or app installation. Both pages stay open during a transfer. Desktop Chrome and Edge support files up to 100 GB, while actual speed depends on the devices and network.
