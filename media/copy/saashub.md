## Tagline

Move large files between phones and computers without an account or app.

## Description

8ma Quick Transfer is a free browser tool for sending files from one device to another. Choose a file, then give the receiver a temporary link, QR code, or 8-character pickup code. When both devices use the same WiFi, they can open the site and use nearby sharing instead.

No account or installation is required. Both pages stay open during the transfer, and speed depends on the devices and current network. Desktop Chrome and Edge support files up to 100 GB.
