Large file transfer

How do you transfer large files quickly?

Skip the wait for a cloud upload and a second download. 8ma Quick Transfer starts sending from the device that holds the file, uses the available network bandwidth, and works without an app or account.

The short answer

Open 8ma Quick Transfer on both devices, choose the file, then use Nearby Sharing, a link, QR code, or 8-character pickup code. Keep both pages open during transfer.

Start in three steps

  1. Open 8ma Quick Transfer on the sending device and choose the file.
  2. Use Nearby Sharing on the same WiFi, or send the link, QR code, or pickup code.
  3. The receiver confirms the download, and both pages stay open until it finishes.

Why is it useful for large files?

There is no separate cloud upload to finish first. Speed, time remaining, and receiver progress stay visible. Desktop Chrome and Edge support files up to 100 GB.

When does it fit best?

  • Sending high-resolution video, design work, project files, or large archives.
  • Moving a file between computers without installing another app.
  • Sending photos and videos between a computer and phone.

What should you check first?

Actual speed depends on both devices and their available network bandwidth. For very large files, use the current desktop Chrome or Edge and keep both devices awake.
