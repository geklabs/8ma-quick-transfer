Text you can quote

One line

8ma Quick Transfer is a free browser file transfer tool with no sign-up, using temporary links, QR codes, 8-character pickup codes, and same-WiFi sharing.

Short description

8ma Quick Transfer helps people move files between phones and computers. Pick a file, then share a temporary link, QR code, or 8-character pickup code. On the same WiFi, open the site on both devices to discover the share. It is free and requires no account or app installation.

Verified facts
  • Free to use, with no sign-up or app installation
  • Temporary link, QR code, and 8-character pickup code
  • Same-WiFi sharing between phones and computers
  • Up to three receivers per share
  • Up to 100 GB per file on desktop Chrome or Edge
  • Actual speed depends on the devices and network; both pages must stay open
